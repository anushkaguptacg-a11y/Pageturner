About the Author

Matt Haig

Matt Haig is the internationally bestselling author of the memoir Reasons to Stay Alive, along with six highly acclaimed novels, including How to Stop Time and The Midnight Library. His work has been translated into over forty languages and has sold millions of copies worldwide. He lives in Brighton with his family and is a vocal advocate for mental health awareness.
